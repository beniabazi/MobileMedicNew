FAQ

Have questions? Check out our frequently asked questions to learn more about our repair process and services.

1. How long does a typical repair take?

Most repairs are completed in under an hour for on-site services. Mail-in repairs are usually processed and returned within 3-5 business days.

2. What types of phones do you repair?

We repair most major phone brands, including iPhones, Samsung, Google Pixel, and more. Contact us if you're unsure if your device is covered.

3. Is there a warranty on the repairs?

Yes, all of our repairs come with a 6-month warranty covering parts and labor. If you experience any issues after the repair, we will fix it free of charge.

4. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, credit cards, Afterpay and major mobile payment services such as Apple Pay and Google Pay. Payments can be made upon completion of the repair for on-site services or online for mail-in repairs.

5. How do I prepare my phone for a mail-in repair?

Before sending your phone, ensure you back up your data, remove any personal information, and disable any security features like Find My iPhone or Google’s Find My Device. Package your phone securely to avoid damage during transit.

6. Can I cancel or reschedule my repair appointment?

Yes, you can cancel or reschedule your appointment up to 24 hours before the scheduled time without any charge. Just contact us, and we’ll be happy to assist.

Warranty Terms & Conditions

At Mobile Medic, we are committed to providing the highest quality of service. All repairs performed by our technicians come with a 6-month limited warranty. This warranty covers defects in parts and workmanship related to the repair performed on your device.

What Our Warranty Covers:

  • Replacement parts used during the repair process, including screens, batteries, and internal components.
  • Labor costs associated with resolving any issues related to the original repair.

What Our Warranty Does Not Cover:

  • Any new damage caused by physical impact, water exposure, or mishandling after the repair has been completed.
  • Issues unrelated to the original repair, such as damage to other components.
  • Malfunctions caused by software issues or third-party modifications.

How to Claim Your Warranty:

If you experience an issue with your repaired device that falls under the warranty coverage, please contact us immediately at admin@mobilemedic.au or call us at +61 466 855 790. We will assess the problem and, if eligible, offer a repair at no additional cost.

Please note that the warranty is only valid for the specific repair performed and does not extend to other parts of the device.
